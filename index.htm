THE CHILLING WORLD FARMSTAY

Lighting your green life

COMMING SOON...

Location

Ba Na - Suoi Mo Street, Hoa Phu Village, Hoa Vang District, Danang City

"The Chilling World Farmstay" wishes to bring that "green living" experience to all visitors. Through connecting people with nature, the climate, the environment and animals. "Green life" is for everyone, especially for tourists when coming to this place.

FarmStay Architecture

With architecture inspired by traditional beauty combined with the countryside of Vietnam, The Chilling World Farmstay will be a new place that visitors are looking for. Those typical architectural styles will be reflected in the specific facilities in this farmstay.

Agriculture

Agriculture

With a large area divided into separate zones, visitors will be guided and experience a day of becoming a farmer at farmstay, including gardening, watering, planting, and taking care of vegetable gardens. Visitors can also gain knowledge of orchid varieties, care techniques, and especially the stories of each orchid.

Services

The Chilling World Farmstay promises to bring customers the most wonderful and unique experiences through the services they receive.

  • Wellness programs

    There are yoga and meditation workshops to help visitors relax all concerns and stress produced by daily problems. A teacher will teach the fundamentals of yoga or meditation to guests.

  • Outdoor activities

    We'll have special activities such as gardening, gardening and caring for plants, caring for animals, swimming, and English summer camp programs

  • Swimming pool

    The swimming pool at The Chilling World Farmstay is for all visitors to come here to visit and stay, with a length of 15m and a width of 7m, a total area of ​​105m² ensuring all the criteria of a standard swimming pool.

  • Kid Club

    Our Kid Club will not be like a kindergarten at farmstay but will be a miniature playground dedicated to "child tourists". With the purpose of providing children with fun experiences through their parents' efforts of recreating and organising childhood folk games.

  • Summer camp

    Nowadays, schools and English centers often organize summer camps for students. With the hot climate this summer, a cool environment combined with an English learning model, and the experience of being a real farmer on a closed farm, is the ideal choice for children.

  • Acoustic show

    Every Saturday there will be a music show. Visitors will enjoy gentle songs by the city's indie bands or the calm melodies of violin and acoustic ensembles.

  • SPA

    Coming to "The Chilling World Farmstay", visitors will experience one of the special services here - Spa. Located on the premises of Farmstay, the herbal bath at the spa brings you the unique ancient and cozy space of the garden house, surrounded by a dense garden with a peaceful green atmosphere. The peaceful scenery combined with the experience of herbal sauna with essential oils like green tea, ginger, and jasmine will bring a completely different relaxation experience, bringing customers closer to nature and the mountains.
